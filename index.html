Elfin IFO Platform

As a gaming platform and hub, Elfin Kingdom Arcade will support traditional and crypto mini-games to be onboarded to the Elfin Kingdom ecosystem, and provide a launchpad for their tokens and NFTs through Elfin Kingdom Farm by IFOs (Initial Farm Offerings).

For in-game assets and IPs, Elfin Kingdom will support the lease and shared use of Elfin IPs for selected games that have submitted their applications.
